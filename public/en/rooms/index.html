Our Rooms

Cebu Apas

Bustling city life, lively and vibrant atmosphere.

Our guesthouse at Apas is a comfortable home in the center of a very bustling community. It is safe and cozy—a perfect place to relax while the neighborhood is teeming with life and activity.

There are many kinds of local restaurants and cafes nearby, so you can always enjoy the tastes of the philippines.

All rooms are single rooms and have their own bathrooms. There is a single bed, a desk and a chair for your convenience. There is also an air conditioner, a fan, and a wardrobe.

Our guesthouse provides accommodation with a garden, private parking and a terrace, which enables you to relax even in the center of metro Cebu city.

If you want to go the mall, Ayala Center Cebu is only 15 minutes away. If you do not have Philippine currency, there are a lot of money changers inside the mall. There are also many places to see nearby since the guesthouse is near to Cebu IT Park.

IT Park is a business district that never sleeps and is filled with many restaurants and shops. If you want to enjoy a quiet and more laid-back environment, you can go for a walk inside Camp Lapu-Lapu and enjoy the scenery.

Cebu Guadalupe

English Only Policy

Simple, sweet, and serene living.

Away from the busy streets of Cebu City, enjoy a calm and tranquil environment in the guesthouse. Our house has a big living room where you can relax and have a fun conversation with the other guests.

You may also unwind and read books in the spacious living room or, enjoy connecting online with the strong WIFI connection and chat with your family and friends. You have access to all common areas.

There are some local restaurants nearby, not only that, a grate cafe with a varistor from UK is just next to our guesthouse.

The nearest Japanese or Korean or Local bars, cafés, and restaurants are only a few blocks away from the place. The nearest mall can be reached in less than 20 minutes.

All rooms are single rooms and have their own bathrooms. There is a single bed, a desk and a chair for your convenience. There is also an air conditioner, a fan, and a wardrobe.

Our guesthouse provides accommodation with a garden, private parking and a terrace, which enables you to relax even in the center of metro Cebu city.

There’s also a lot of transport vehicles you can ride on to-and-from the place. You can hail a taxi where the base rate is as low as 40PHP or ride a jeepney just for 8PHP.

Or if you want to do it the local way, you can back ride on a motorcycle called “habal-habal” for as low as 50PHP.
